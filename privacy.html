Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Destro ("Destro", "we", "our", or "us") provides warehouse orchestration and automation software designed to operate within customer-controlled environments. This Privacy Policy describes how Destro handles data in connection with our software and services.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Destro software platform, including backend orchestration services, wearable interfaces, administrative dashboards, and related operational systems.

Destro is an enterprise software provider and operates primarily as a data processor on behalf of its customers.

2. Data We Process

Destro processes operational data necessary to provide warehouse orchestration and automation functionality. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., wearable device IDs)
  • Operational workflow data
  • Robot and system telemetry
  • System logs and audit records
  • Configuration and deployment data

Destro does not collect or process personal consumer data such as names, email addresses, financial information, or personal communications.

3. Customer-Controlled Data Environment

Destro software is deployed within infrastructure environments controlled by our customers. Customers retain ownership and control of their data at all times.

Destro does not access customer data except as necessary to provide support, troubleshoot issues, or improve system functionality, and only with appropriate authorization.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Operational data is stored only as required for system functionality, monitoring, and operational continuity. Data retention policies are controlled by the customer’s infrastructure and deployment configuration.

Destro does not independently retain customer operational data outside of customer-controlled environments unless explicitly authorized.

5. Data Security

Destro implements industry-standard security controls to protect data, including:

  • Encrypted communication using TLS
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure deployment and container isolation
  • Audit logging and system monitoring
  • Vulnerability scanning and patch management

Access to customer systems and data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Sharing

Destro does not sell, rent, or share customer data with third parties.

Customer data remains within customer-controlled infrastructure environments unless explicitly authorized by the customer.

7. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for managing access controls, retention policies, and security configurations within their deployment environments.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
