Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Destro ("Destro", "we", "our", or "us") provides warehouse orchestration and automation software designed to operate within customer-controlled infrastructure environments. This Privacy Policy describes how Destro handles data in connection with our software and services.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Destro software platform, including backend orchestration services, wearable interfaces, administrative dashboards, and related operational systems.

Destro operates as an enterprise software provider and processes operational data solely to provide system functionality on behalf of its customers.

2. Data We Process

Destro processes operational and system data necessary for warehouse orchestration and automation. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., wearable device IDs)
  • Operational workflow and task execution data
  • Robot and system telemetry
  • System logs and audit records
  • Configuration and deployment information

Destro does not collect or process personal consumer data such as names, email addresses, financial information, or personal communications.

3. Customer-Controlled Data Environment

Destro software is deployed within infrastructure environments controlled and managed by customers. Customers retain ownership and control of their data at all times.

Destro does not access customer data except as necessary to provide authorized support, troubleshoot operational issues, or improve system reliability, and only with appropriate authorization.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Operational data is stored only as required for active system functionality, monitoring, and operational continuity. Data retention policies are governed by customer infrastructure and deployment configuration.

Destro does not independently retain customer operational data outside of customer-controlled environments unless explicitly authorized.

5. Data Security

Destro implements industry-standard security controls to protect system and operational data, including:

  • Encrypted communication using TLS
  • Authentication and access controls
  • Secure containerized deployment and network isolation
  • System logging, monitoring, and audit trails
  • Dependency and vulnerability scanning

Access to systems and operational data is restricted to authorized personnel and controlled through secure authentication mechanisms.

6. Data Sharing

Destro does not sell, rent, or share customer operational data with third parties.

Customer data remains within customer-controlled infrastructure environments unless explicitly authorized by the customer.

7. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for managing access controls, retention policies, network security, and operational security configurations within their deployment environments.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
